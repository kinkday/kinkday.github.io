About KinkDay

KinkDay is a random sexual act generator. KinkDay has a predefined list of over 100 existing kinky acts that will randomly be displayed.

It was created out of pure boredom, help from a friend, and inspiration from another.

It is completely secure and pulls no data from our users. No cookies are stored and no logs are taken by us. If you are interested in verifying this, the source code is available at https://github.com/kinkday/kinkday.github.io for you to view.

The site will be periodically updated. If you are interested in suggesting new kinky acts, visit https://kinkday.github.io/new.html and fill out the form.
